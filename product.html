Our product

Our concept consists of a product and a service. The tool is a wearable bracelet, both for men and women (see figures above for a sketch of the concept). The bracelet comes in gold and silver and includes an alarm speaker, bluetooth connection to the application and a flashlight. The bracelet is wirelessly rechargeable and water-resistant.< /p>

To activate the bracelet’s features on the product itself and on the application, the bracelet includes two buttons: one button for the device features and one button for the application features. Different fast and long pushes on the bracelet’s buttons will trigger different features of the bracelet and the application.

The design of the bracelet is simple yet modern to attract people of all age categories and to be able to use the bracelet daily.
