DefendME

A study by Knoema, a world data bank, shows that Spain’s assault rate is 39.2 cases per 100,000 inhabitants and the forceful robbery rate is 129.1 cases per 100,000 inhabitants. Both rates apply to the year 2018 but have been increasing over the years. As this study and other studies, articles and reality show, a defense tool is needed in our current time and space as the chance to get assaulted or robbed are very likely. Moreover, during recent years, the number of testimonials of victims of violent and/or sexual assault in the streets or at home or in the workplace have been increasing tremendously. Each day, a new case of assault to a member of a minority group is reported, showing the need for actions that should be taken to avoid these assaults from happening
