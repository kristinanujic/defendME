Product Description

The bracelet can be worn by anyone, and comes in a variety of colours, with a choice of stone colour. It includes an alarm speaker, Bluetooth connection to the application and a flashlight. The bracelet is wirelessly rechargeable and water-resistant. To activate the bracelet’s features on the product itself and on the application, the bracelet includes two buttons: one button for the bracelet’s features and one button for the application’s features. Different fast and long pushes on the bracelet’s buttons will activate the different features of the bracelet and the application.
