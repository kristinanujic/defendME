Mission Statement

Our company’s mission is to develop and market a product to help people feel safer and protect themselves in situations in which they feel at risk without harming others.

Vision Statement

To make people regain their power and confidence by providing a self-defence mechanism for all. As our customers will keep our product with them at all times and it will show that their confidence gets a boost due to the power of the features of the bracelet and the community it creates, more and more people will buy our bracelet to regain their confidence and a safer community will exist due to the perpetrator’s and general population’s knowledge that it has become more difficult to attack people for all kinds of reasons whether it’s theft, sexual assault or other. Moreover, once our business will be (internationally) thriving, one big safety community will be created with the majority of citizens acknowledging the need for prevention of the cause of the problem instead of solving the problem after. As citizens are the true power of a city, a country and the world, leaders will have no choice but to follow and act on the pressing matters that their citizens have brought to light. Hence, making the world a safer place for everyone.
