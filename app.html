DefendME app

Our bracelet connects to the customer’s phone via bluetooth, without eliminating or disturbing other bluetooth connections. The bracelet connects with the application on the phone, which consists of a freemium version and a subscription version which offers valuable extra features for a monthly fee.

The application adds significant value to our product by alarming people of the victim’s situation, faking a phone call to deter assaulters, recording audio and gps location to provide evidence of assault to police and locking and alarming phone in case of robbery among other things.
